**Continuum Risk** are an insurance company, who wants to leverage Alteryx to support their reserving team with their quarterly financial close processes. They have recently undergone a data modernization project whereby the source data for this project have been loaded into Databricks.

## Objective

Provide an accelerated view of expected reserves ahead of month-end close by combining the latest available premiums and claims data, applying agreed reserving methodologies, and highlighting movements versus prior periods.

## Scope and Process

### Data Ingestion & Integration

- Merge the latest available earned premium and claims data from Databricks
- Align data to a common structure by underwriting period, accident period, loss type and reinsurance type
- Convert all values from local currency into USD

## Business Value

- Provides earlier visibility into reserve movements and emerging risks
- Reduces pressure at month-end by identifying issues ahead of Full Close
- Balances automation with actuarial judgement through controlled overrides
- Improves confidence and preparedness for final financial close
